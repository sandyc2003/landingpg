About


On 18 September 1965, Samyang was established under the name Lotte Food Industrial Company in Seoul, South Korea by Shin Choon-ho, brother of Lotte Corporation founder Shin Kyuk-ho. When Samyang introduced its first ramyun, Lotte Ramyun, in 1965, there were 7 other companies in the market. As a second mover in the ramyun industry, Samyang focused on research and development. Along with South Korea's first commercialized snack, Shrimp Cracker (Hangul: 새우깡; Romanization: Saewookang; 1971), Beef Ramyun (Hangul: 소고기라면; 1970), and Samyang Ramyun (1975), Samyang achieved 35% market share in the mid-1970s. On 6 March 1978, Samyang changed its name from Lotte Food Industrial Company to Samyang Co., Ltd
